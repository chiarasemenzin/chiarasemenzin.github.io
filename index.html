Chiara Semenzin

I am a linguist and cognitive scientist. My research focuses on how AI can help us understand non-human communication.

After earning my degree in Linguistics (MA) and Cognitive Science (MSc) from the University of Edinburgh, I am conducting interdisciplinary research by exploring the intersection of linguistics, informatics, and biology.

Currently, I am a PhD researcher at ENS Paris, where I study dolphin communication, using tools from AI and maths to decode patterns in their vocalizations. Beyond my research, I am committed to science outreach and always fostering connections between disciplines, and people!
